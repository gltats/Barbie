Cast

Margot Robbie

Barbara Handler, formerly known as Barbie and also known as Stereotypical Barbie, is the main protagonist of Barbie: The Movie. She is played by Margot Robbie. Barbie lives in a Dream House in Barbie Land. After experiencing an existential crisis, she and Ken went to the Real World together, eventually leading to her becoming human.

Ryan Gosling

Ken is one of the main characters and former antagonist in Barbie: The Movie. He is played by Ryan Gosling. Having an unrequited crush on Stereotypical Barbie, he joined her on her quest to the real world. After spending some time in the real world, Ken learned about the system of patriarchy, going back to Barbie Land to export it there. His plans were eventually thwarted, and he learned to accept himself without having to define himself by his relationship to Barbie.

Ariadna Greenblatt

Sasha is a character in Barbie: The Movie. She is played by Ariana Greenblatt. Sasha lives in the Real World with her mother Gloria.

Will Ferrel

The Mattel CEO is a fictional character in Barbie: The Movie. He is played by Will Ferrell. He is the Mattel CEO in the Real World.

Emma Mackey

Barbie is a character in Barbie: The Movie. She is played by Emma Mackey. Barbie is a famous physicist who won a Nobel Prize, and she lives in Barbie Land.

Simu Liu

Ken is a character in Barbie: The Movie. He is played by Simu Liu. He had a rivalry with Ken.

Michael Cera

Allan is a character in Barbie: The Movie. He is played by Canadian actor Michael Cera. Allan lives in Barbie Land. There is only one Allan, and he is Ken's buddy. Allan is observed to be different than the other Kens, hence the name. Allan seems to be awkward and out of place, and gives off the impression that he's not the biggest fan of Barbie Land. After Ken changes Barbie Land, he even tags along with Sasha & Gloria as they return to the Real World.

Kate McKinnon

Barbie is a character in Barbie: The Movie. She is played by Kate McKinnon. She is known as Weird Barbie. Weird Barbie helps Barbie get through to the normal world and also helps the other Barbies snap out of Ken's ideal mindset. Weird Barbie is based off of when people play with their Barbies a little too hard, such as drawing on their face, cutting up their hair or placing them into weird positions.

America Ferrera

Gloria is a character in Barbie: The Movie. Gloria has a daughter named Sasha. Gloria meets Barbie in the Real World. She is portrayed by America Ferrera.

Issa Rae

Sypnosis
